sezar salat

Caesar salad is a fresh, crunchy dish made with romaine lettuce, crispy croutons, and a creamy, tangy dressing usually made from Parmesan cheese, garlic, lemon juice, and anchovies. It's often topped with grilled chicken for extra protein

preparation time

  • total:40-60 minits
  • preparation:20 minits
  • cooking:30 minits

ingrediens

  • larde pices of lettuse
  • bread
  • Parmesan cheese

instruction

1. Wash and chop the romaine lettuce into bite-sized pieces.
2. Toast some bread cubes to make crispy croutons.
3. Grate fresh Parmesan cheese or cut it into thin slices.
4. In a bowl, mix the dressing: garlic, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, anchovy (optional), egg yolk or mayo, olive oil, and Parmesan.
5. Whisk the dressing until smooth and creamy.
6. In a large bowl, combine the lettuce and croutons.
7. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss well.
8. Top with more Parmesan and add grilled chicken if desired
